China Qualified Investors

Enquiring about
our China funds?

Access to our Chinese quantitative fund series is restricted to qualified investors under China's private fund regulations — including individuals and institutions meeting the relevant net asset or income thresholds.

If you are a qualified investor interested in our quantitative fund products, please submit an enquiry using the form above and select "China Quantitative Funds" as your area of interest. Our Shanghai team will be in touch with detailed product information and onboarding documentation.

✦ Individual minimum investment: RMB 1,000,000 per product
✦ Individual financial assets must exceed RMB 3,000,000, or average annual income must exceed RMB 500,000 over the last three years
✦ Institutional investors with net assets of at least RMB 10,000,000 qualify automatically
✦ Social security funds, enterprise annuity funds, charitable funds, and AMAC-supervised investment plans are automatically qualified
