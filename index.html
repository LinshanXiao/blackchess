Who We Are

A new kind of
investment firm.

Blackchess Capital is built on the conviction that the world's most compelling investment opportunities sit at the intersection of two of its fastest-moving economies — Australia and China.

We operate a dual-market platform: a venture capital arm investing in early-stage Australian companies across disruptive technology, hospitality and entertainment, and alternative energy; and a quantitative division deploying AI-driven strategies across Chinese equity and futures markets.
